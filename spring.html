Spring is where the children's work lives.

Spring is the children's literature and education branch of The Museum of Brilliance. Picture books, supporting curriculum, and quiet tools for small, curious readers.

§ One

The approach

Spring serves parents and Reggio- and Montessori-aligned educators equally. The books are made with care. The supporting materials are made with restraint. Both are intended to last on a shelf — not to be consumed and discarded.

The flagship title, Barnaby Bites: Spaghetti Day, is in development. Additional titles and companion units will follow.
